🧠 What This Teaches Us

Professional editors read AI drafts with three questions running simultaneously: Does this sound right? (tone), Is this clear? (clarity), and Is this true? (accuracy). AI often gets one or two right but misses the third. Developing the habit of checking all three — even in a 60-second scan — is what separates a professional from someone who just hits send.
