🧠 Oversight Is Something You Design

The biggest mistake companies make with AI is assuming they can add oversight later — after a problem appears. The most responsible AI systems have human checkpoints built into the workflow from the beginning. Knowing where to put those checkpoints — at the highest-stakes moments, not just the most obvious ones — is a skill that makes you valuable in any organization using AI tools.
