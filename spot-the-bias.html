🧠 Why Human Review of AI Content Matters

AI systems learn from huge amounts of text and images created by humans — which means they can absorb and repeat the biases that exist in that data. AI does not understand fairness the way a person does. It cannot feel the impact of a stereotype or know when a group has historically been left out. That is why human reviewers like you are essential. Every piece of AI-generated marketing content should be checked before it reaches a real audience.
