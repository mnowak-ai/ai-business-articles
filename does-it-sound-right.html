⏱️ The 60-Second Rule

Before you send anything — especially a message drafted with AI — pause for sixty seconds and ask yourself just two things: Does the tone match the audience? And would the reader know exactly what to do? AI can get you most of the way there. That final check is what makes the difference between a message that just gets sent and one that actually lands.
