Risk Summary
What Human Oversight Actually Catches

🧠 The Responsibility Behind the Tool

Every AI output in a real business has a human on the other end — someone whose job, loan, health, or understanding of the world is affected by whether the AI got it right. That is why oversight is not optional. The people who use AI professionally are not just users — they are the last line of quality control between the algorithm and the real world. Developing that instinct — to always ask "what happens if this is wrong?" — is one of the most valuable habits you can build.
