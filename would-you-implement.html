🧠 The Core Lesson

AI recommendations are built on data patterns — but data does not capture everything. It misses context, relationships, timing, and the human factors that make or break a business decision. The best decision-makers use AI as their first draft — a strong starting point that still needs your judgment, your experience, and your knowledge of things the data cannot see.
