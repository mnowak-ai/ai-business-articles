🧠 Ethics in Action

Ethical AI review is not about being suspicious of technology — it is about being responsible with it. Every AI output that affects a real person deserves a human who asks: "Is this fair? Is this complete? Could this hurt someone?" Developing that reflex — especially when the output looks fine at first glance — is what separates a thoughtful professional from someone who just clicks approve.
