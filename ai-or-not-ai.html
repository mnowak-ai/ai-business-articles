🧠 The Big Idea

Not every business task belongs to AI — and not every task needs a human. The smartest business owners learn to tell the difference. Routine and repeatable tasks? AI handles those faster and cheaper. Tasks that need creativity, judgment, or emotional intelligence? That is where humans are irreplaceable. And the middle ground — AI drafting, human reviewing — is often the most powerful combination of all.
