🧠 Why This Matters in the Real World

AI note-taking tools can save a lot of time — but they make mistakes like these regularly. They sometimes assign tasks to the wrong person, invent deadlines that were never mentioned, miss action items that were brought up casually, and write vague commitments with no clear owner. In a real workplace, sending out notes with these errors can confuse your team, miss important follow-ups, or even damage client relationships. Always review AI output before sharing it.
