🧠 What AI Looks For When Planning Your Day

A good AI day-planning tool doesn't just look at deadlines — it considers urgency (how soon does this need action?), importance (how much does this affect your goals?), and who else is involved. Tasks that block other people or are client-facing usually rise to the top. Low-impact interruptions get filtered out so you can stay focused.
