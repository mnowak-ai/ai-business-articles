🧠 How AI Builds Personas in Real Life

Real marketing AI tools — like HubSpot and Adobe Sensei — build personas by analyzing thousands of data points: purchase history, search terms, social media activity, and customer reviews. They look for patterns and group customers who behave similarly. But here is the key: AI spots the patterns, and humans decide what they mean. The judgment you practiced here — reading clues and deciding what a customer really cares about — is something AI cannot fully replace.
