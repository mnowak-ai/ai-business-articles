🧠 What AI Can — and Can't — Do for Project Planning

Real AI tools used in business — like Asana or Monday.com — work a lot like this simulation. They look at your project schedule, spot risks before they become problems, and suggest options for you to consider. But they don't make the final call — you do. That's because AI doesn't know your team's energy level, your client's personality, or the relationships your company has built over time. The best project managers use AI to think faster and spot problems earlier — but they still bring their own judgment to every decision.
