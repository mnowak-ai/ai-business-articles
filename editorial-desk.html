🧠 Why Editing AI Output Is a Professional Skill

AI summaries like this one are written in seconds — but they regularly contain all three types of problems you just looked for. Real marketing professionals, journalists, and analysts check AI output for accuracy (is this fact correct?), fairness (is one side being left out?), and clarity (will the reader actually understand this?). The goal is not to distrust AI — it is to use it as a first draft, not a final answer.
